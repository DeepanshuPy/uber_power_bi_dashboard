# 📊 Uber Power BI Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ride-sharing data and generate business insights across bookings, revenue, customers, vehicles, and locations.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To build a business-ready analytics solution that enables stakeholders to:
- Track key performance metrics
- Identify revenue drivers
- Understand customer behavior
- Optimize operations

---

## 🔢 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** 52M  
- **Completed Bookings:** 93K  
- **Lost Bookings:** 57K  
- **Total Distance:** 3M  
- **Avg Distance:** 24.6 km  

---

## 🚀 Key Insights

- **Top Revenue Drivers**
  - Auto: 12.9M  
  - Bike: 11.5M  
  → ~47% of total revenue

- **Performance Drop**
  - February bookings dropped to ~9K  
  → Direct impact on revenue

- **Payment Trends**
  - UPI dominates (~37K bookings / ~23M revenue)

- **Peak Demand Time**
  - 6 PM – 9 PM (~4.7K rides)

- **Top Locations**
  - Khandsa (949)  
  - Barakhamba (946)  
  - Saket (931)

- **Vehicle Insight**
  - Uber XL has very low contribution (~0.07M distance)

- **Cancellations**
  - Blank reasons (~29K)  
  - Customer issues (~6.8K)  
  → Indicates data quality & operational gaps

---

## 📂 Dashboard Pages

- **Overview:** KPIs, trends, revenue breakdown  
- **Vehicle:** Performance by vehicle type  
- **Revenue:** Revenue by customer, payment method  
- **Rider:** Customer behavior & cancellations  
- **Location:** Demand areas, peak time slots
